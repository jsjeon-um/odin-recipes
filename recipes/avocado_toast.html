Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado Toast is a type of sandwich consisting of toast with mashed avocado, and usually salt, black pepper, and sometimes citrus juice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 small avocado
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/2 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, Maldon sea salt flakes

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Gently mash with the back of a fork.
  2. Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle over desired toppings.
